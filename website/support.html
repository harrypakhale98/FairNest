Include the iPhone model, iOS version, FairNest version, whether iCloud Sync is on, and a short description of what happened. Do not include private household card text, partner details, screenshots, or exports unless they are necessary to explain the issue.

Common Fixes

  • For iCloud Sync or partner sharing issues, confirm the device is signed in to iCloud and that iCloud Drive is available.
  • For reminders, open FairNest Settings and check notification permission before scheduling gentle reminders again.
  • For widget issues, open FairNest once after editing cards so the widget snapshot can refresh.
  • For privacy requests, use FairNest's Privacy screen to export local data, delete local data, or delete shared household data where CloudKit permissions allow it.

Privacy

This support page is static. It has no form, cookies, analytics, advertising scripts, or custom backend. If you email support, the message is handled by your email provider and the developer's email provider so the developer can respond.
