## Description

FairNest is a private household planning app for turning invisible home work into clear, shared next steps.

Capture a messy brain dump, review suggested cards before anything is saved, and keep household responsibilities organized on a simple home board. Use weekly check-ins to notice what felt heavy, what got done, what needs clearer ownership, and one appreciation worth keeping.

FairNest is local-first. iCloud Sync is off by default, partner sharing is optional, and raw brain dump text is never automatically shared. When you choose to enable iCloud, FairNest uses Apple CloudKit and Apple's private sharing flow so household cards can be shared with invited participants.

Key features:
- Home board for tasks, recurring responsibilities, decisions, and appreciations.
- Brain dump suggestions that stay reviewable before saving.
- Weekly check-in flow for ownership and appreciation.
- Local reminders for due work and check-ins.
- Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets.
- Privacy controls to export local data, delete local data, and delete shared household data where your iCloud permissions allow it.

FairNest has no ads, subscriptions, in-app purchases, third-party analytics, custom backend, or paid API dependency.

## Review Notes Draft

FairNest is free and has no ads, subscriptions, in-app purchases, custom backend, third-party analytics, or paid API dependency.

The app is local-first. iCloud Sync is off by default and uses CloudKit only when enabled by the user. Partner sharing is optional and uses Apple's private CloudKit sharing sheet. Brain dump suggestions are prepared on device and are user-reviewed before saving. Local notifications are used only after permission is granted.

FairNest is not therapy, counseling, mental-health treatment, medical advice, legal advice, or diagnosis.
